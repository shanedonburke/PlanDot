The shows all of the items you've created. On large screens, each item is shown next to its description.

On touch screens, you can swipe left on an item to reveal a button that deletes the item. Tapping anywhere else on the screen will hide the button.

Like in the , you can drag_handle drag items to reorder them.

You may also search for items by typing keywords in the search bar. Try entering item titles, dates, description keywords, locations, and group names!
